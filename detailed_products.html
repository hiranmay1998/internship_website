Amrit Suraksha – Pure Term Insurance Plan

Best For: Providing affordable financial protection for your family in case something happens to you. It’s the easiest and most cost-effective way to ensure your loved ones are financially secure.

  • Coverage: ₹50 Lakh to ₹2 Crore
  • Premium: Starts at just ₹450 per month (for a 30-year-old, non-smoker, ₹1 Crore coverage).
  • Policy Term: Choose how long you want the policy to last – 10 years, 20 years, or up to 40 years.
  • Payout: If something happens to you, your family will receive a lump sum amount as the death benefit.

Riders Available:

  • Accidental Death Benefit – Extra coverage in case of accidental death.
  • Critical Illness Rider – Provides coverage if you’re diagnosed with a critical illness like cancer, heart attack, etc.
  • Waiver of Premium – If you become critically ill or face certain challenges, you won’t have to pay premiums for the rest of the policy term.

Tax Benefits:

  • Under Section 80C: Premiums paid are tax-deductible.
  • Under Section 10(10D): Death benefits are tax-free.

Why Choose This Plan:

This plan offers affordable protection without the financial burden. With Amrit Suraksha, you can ensure your family’s future, no matter what happens, and at a very low cost. It’s flexible, easy to understand, and provides peace of mind to the policyholder and their family.

Amrit Nivesh – Savings + Protection Plan

Best For: A perfect blend of life insurance and savings. Not only does it provide financial protection, but it also helps you build wealth over time.

  • Coverage: This plan combines life coverage and a guaranteed maturity benefit (for example, ₹10 Lakh after 20 years).
  • Premium: ₹2,000 to ₹5,000 per month, depending on the sum you choose and how long you want to invest.
  • Policy Term: 10, 15, or 20 years

Returns:

  • At the end of the term, you will receive a lump sum amount that is guaranteed, along with any accrued bonuses.
  • Bonus Feature: The policy may offer additional bonuses based on the company’s performance, adding extra value to your investment.

Flexibility:

  • Choose how you want to pay – you can go for a one-time single premium or regular premium payments (monthly, quarterly, or annually).
  • Opt for limited pay, where you pay premiums for a shorter period but still enjoy long-term benefits.

Tax Benefits:

  • You can get tax deductions under Section 80C for premiums paid.
  • The maturity amount and death benefits are also tax-free under Section 10(10D).

Why Choose This Plan:

This plan is ideal for those planning for future milestones like buying a home, getting married, or setting up a business. It not only gives you life cover but also helps you build a financial cushion with guaranteed returns. So, you can stay protected while growing your wealth.

Amrit Bal Suraksha – Child Education Plan

Best For: Securing your child’s future by ensuring they have the money they need for education, even if you're not around.

  • Coverage: Provides financial support to fund your child’s education at important milestones (like school, college, or higher education).
  • Also provides life cover, ensuring your child’s education is not disrupted even if something happens to you.
  • Premium: Starts at ₹1,500 per month, based on your child’s age and the amount of coverage you choose.
  • Policy Term: 10 to 25 years – depending on your child’s age and when you want the policy to mature.

Key Features:

  • Premium Waiver: If something happens to you, the premiums will be waived off, but your child will still get the benefits.
  • Guaranteed Education Payouts: As your child reaches certain educational stages, you will receive payouts to cover tuition and other related costs.

Bonuses:

  • Loyalty Additions: Additional benefits will be added to your policy, the longer you stay invested.
  • Terminal Bonus: A special bonus added at the end of the term, based on the performance of the policy.

Tax Benefits:

  • Premiums are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80C.
  • Death benefits are tax-free under Section 10(10D).

Why Choose This Plan:

Even if you’re not around, this plan ensures that your child gets the support they need at each important step of their education. Whether it’s primary school, higher education, or professional courses, Amrit Bal Suraksha ensures your child’s future is bright and secure.

Amrit Varishtha – Retirement Pension Plan

Best For: Securing a steady, guaranteed income after you retire, so you can enjoy your golden years without financial worries.

  • Coverage: Provides a lifetime annuity payout starting from your retirement.
  • Premium: ₹5,000 per month for 20 years can give you a pension of ₹25,000–₹30,000 per month* (based on your age and policy details).

Annuity Options:

  • Life Annuity: Receive a regular income for life.
  • Life with Return of Purchase Price: Receive a regular income, and after your death, the premium paid will be returned to your nominee.
  • Joint Life Annuity: If you’re married, you can choose an annuity for you and your spouse, ensuring both of you get regular income.

Policy Term:

  • You can choose a deferment period of 10–30 years (this is the waiting time before the payout starts).
  • The annuity payout will continue till the end of your life or up to the age of 99.

Tax Benefits:

  • Contributions are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80CCC.
  • The annuity payments you receive are taxed as per your income.

Why Choose This Plan:

This plan is designed to give you peace of mind when you retire. It provides you with a stable income to manage your living expenses and inflation. No more worrying about running out of money in your retirement years – Amrit Varishtha ensures you stay financially independent in your golden years.
